Rommie

Microsoft Design Expo 2012 Best Social Concept

Housing renting is very important for Chinese young people after they finish the collage life. They will suffer a lot to find convenient apartment and companionable roommate during this transition period. We design a solution for the youth to enjoy the new life by integrating the virtual information and physical world together. The final conceptual prototype of Roomie was chosen to present in the Microsoft Design Expo 2012 in Redmond and received the reward of Best Social Concept.

Paintora

provincial level Startup Competition First Prize

For many young children, communicating emotional subjects, even to their parents, is difficult . Paintora is a drawing app inspired by Art Therapy that helps children learn this important skill. Paintora prompts children with psychological cues to help them communicate through drawings. Children can draw freely and express their concerns. A web-based observation platform for parents allows understanding of the child’s work and, more importantly, lets them get to know their child better.
